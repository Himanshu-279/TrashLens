How TrashLens Works

Our AI-powered system makes waste classification simple and accurate in just three steps.

1. Snap

Capture Your Waste

You upload an image of a waste item. Clear images with a simple background work best for the most accurate classification.

  • Upload from device or computer
  • Take a photo instantly

2. Classify

AI Analysis

Our powerful EfficientNetV2 model analyzes the image in seconds, comparing it against thousands of examples to identify the waste type with high accuracy.

3. Act

Get Guidance

The app immediately tells you if the item is recyclable and provides clear, step-by-step instructions for proper disposal.

  • Recyclable or non-recyclable status
  • Step-by-step disposal guide

Recyclable!

Place in the appropriate recycling bin
